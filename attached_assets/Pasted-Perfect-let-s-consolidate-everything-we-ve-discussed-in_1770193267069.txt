## 2. **Core Modules**

### A. **Vehicle Management**
- Vehicle profiles (make, model, year, VIN/plate, mileage).  
- Service history linked to each vehicle.  
- Insurance details and renewal tracking.  
- Maintenance schedules with reminders.  

### B. **Client Management**
- Client directory with contact details (name, phone, email, address).  
- Link clients to their vehicles and service records.  
- Categorize clients (individuals, corporate fleets).  
- Privacy and compliance controls (POPIA/GDPR).  

### C. **Document Repository**
- Central storage for all files (repair records, insurance policies, communiquÃ©s, invoices).  
- Metadata tagging (vehicle ID, client ID, date, type).  
- Full-text search and filters.  
- Version control and archiving.  

### D. **Job & Work Order Management**
- Create and assign repair jobs or maintenance tasks.  
- Track progress and completion.  
- Link jobs to vehicles and clients.  
- Generate invoices automatically from completed jobs.  

### E. **Inventory & Parts Management**
- Track spare parts, consumables, and tools.  
- Monitor stock levels and reorder alerts.  
- Link parts usage to specific jobs.  
- Supplier integration for streamlined ordering.  

### F. **Billing & Finance**
- Generate invoices and receipts.  
- Track payments and outstanding balances.  
- Export data to accounting systems.  
- Financial dashboards (monthly revenue, service costs).  

### G. **Calendar & Scheduling**
- Maintenance schedules for vehicles.

### H. **Client Portal**
- Secure login for clients.  
- View vehicle history, upcoming appointments, and invoices.  
- Online booking for services.  
- Document sharing (insurance policies, service reports).  

### I. **Fleet Management**
- Manage multiple vehicles under one client account.  
- Consolidated reporting for fleet managers.  
- Bulk scheduling and reminders.  

### J. **Mobile Access**
- Mechanics update work orders from tablets/phones.  
- Clients receive notifications and book appointments via mobile.  
- Managers approve jobs or check reports on the go.  

### K. **Analytics & Predictive Maintenance**
- Reports on service frequency, client activity, insurance coverage.  
- Predictive maintenance alerts based on historical data.  
- Insights into client retention and profitability.  

### L. **Staff Communication & Collaboration**
- Internal messaging system (direct messages, group chats).  
- Document sharing tied to jobs, vehicles, or clients.  
- Discussion threads on specific work orders or vehicle profiles.  
- Company-wide announcements/notice board.

## 5. **Processes**
- **Ingestion**: Upload documents, create jobs, add vehicles/clients.  
- **Organization**: Categorize by vehicle, client, job, and document type.  
- **Retrieval**: Search by vehicle ID, client name, job status, or date.  
- **Maintenance**: Archive old records, back up data, compliance checks.  
- **Notifications**: Alerts for service dates, insurance renewals, job deadlines.  
- **Communication**: Staff messaging and document sharing.  
- **Linking**: Every document tied to both a vehicle and a client.
